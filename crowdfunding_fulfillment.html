What is Crowdfunding Fulfillment ?

when a project is successfully funded, the creator is responsible for completing the project and fulfilling each reward to the best of their abilities.Their fundamental obligation to backers is to finish all the work that was promised, honestly address backers' concerns, and deliver rewards.

Crowdfunding creators tend to focus on creativity but not on fulfillment. Most of them prefer to outsource crowdfunding fulfillment to a professional fulfillment company which is a key to streamline and simplify the fulfillment process. GM fulfillment has an in-house system which is specifically built with managing crowdfunding fulfillment in mind. Our intuitive software has a friendly interface and is easy to use.
